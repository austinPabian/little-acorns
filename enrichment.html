CHILD ENRICHMENT

Enrichment Sessions

Learning and developing go hand-in-hand.

Little Acorns understands the value of meaningful enrichment sessions. We are committed to providing a well rounded curriculum to littles. At times, we have special visits from organizations from our local community.

In addition to special visits, we currently have weekly enrichment sessions for storytelling, nature interaction, music, and so much more!

ENRICHMENT FEATURES

Toddleabout Nature Exploration

Toddleabout Nature Exploration is a weekly combination of art and science, allowing children to explore and connect with nature at their own pace. Concepts talked about will include anatomy, life cycle, food web, defense mechanisms, predator-prey relationships, habitat, etc. Children will have opportunity to interact with live animals in a safe and nurturing environment.

Check out her Facebook page here!

Music with Wanda

This is a biweekly engagement of interactive music sessions with Miss Wanda, filled with singing, movement, puppet play, and a wide variety of stringed and rhythm instruments.

Check out her website here!

Stories on the Move®

Stories on the Move® is a monthly Reggio inspired class that features an interactive, musical storytelling and movement journey. Each visit is unquie and a reflection of the current group in attendance. All age levels participate together.

WHAT GROWS AN ACORN?

Cognitive

Engaging activities and rich experiences are presented throughout our day to help develop critical thinking and problem solving skills. Children are encourage to ask questions and seek their own answers through play, experiments, and fact gathering. Some areas of focus include: language, phonetic awareness, science, math, reading, and writing.

Social & Emotional

Children will develop skills to positively interact and socialize with peers and adults. Children are encouraged to develop relationships with mixed ages, identify and express their emotions, and communicate their needs and ideas while being offered ample amount of uninterrupted time for free exploration.

Physical

Daily experiences are provided to help develop a child’s coordination and motor skills. We embrace the need for children to run, jump, climb, and attempt heavy work. We view our playground as an extension of the classroom, spending as much time learning outside as weather allows.

Self-Help & Adaptive

Children are presented with opportunities to recognize and care for their own needs and the needs of their community. Cultivating teachable moments allows for our community to seize the day, allowing children to development a sense of flexibility and joy for learning.

Mindfulness & Peace Education

Self-regulation and self-awareness is weaved throughout our program. Children are presented with opportunities for mindful breathing and yoga. Empathy, peaceful conflict resolution, and care of our planet are critical components to our program.
