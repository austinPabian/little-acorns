Welcome to
Little Acorns!

We are a preschool based in DeWitt, Michigan that practices the Montessori method of education.

This means we are child oriented, child initiated, and teacher supported. We believe all children learn best through hands-on experiences.

Our Foundation

Montessori Practices

Our methods of educating focus on developing independence, confidence, and empathy.

Dedicated Teachers

All of our amazing teachers come to Little Acorns each day because they love what they do.

Eco-consciousness

We aim to eliminate plasticware from meal times and strive for an 80% organic menu.

Accessibile Facilities

Little Acorns is conveniently located on ground level for all children to be able to come and learn.

Hand-Prepared Nourisment

Our menu focuses on freshly prepared meals and engagement at meal time in a "family-style" setting.

secure property

Little Acorns is secured and monitored during all hours of operation by the active staff.

child development

Children are encouraged to explore their environment freely and develop necessary childhood skills.

hands-on learning

Children are presented with materials and are encourage to freely explore and create.
